HOW TO MAKE A DC MOTOR BOAT

This toturial will teach you on how to make your own motorised boat using locally available materials

You will need

A motor

Two black wires and two red or white wires

A propeller

Two AA batteries or[3.6 or 3.7 volt rechargable battery recommended]

A switch

A 40cm by 40cm piece of thermocol

A dry cell holder[two slots and for AA or for a rechargable battery]

Some hot glue or super glue.

insulating tape.


STEPS

Follow the following steps in order to make your boat.

1.Cut the piece of thermocol into a boat-shaped structure and line the edges with some of it.

2.Place some hot glue or super glue at the seams of the boat and let it dry.

3.For the wiring,attach the red/white wire to the positive side of the dry cell holder and do the same with the negative side and the black wire.

4.Attach the white/red wire of the motor to the black wire of the dry cell holder.

5.attach the white/red wire of the dry cell holder to the positive terminal[+] of the switch and attach the black wire of the motor to the negative terminal[-] of the switch.

6.After all the wiring is done,add a propeller and attach it to the tip of the motor.

7.Glue the motor to the end of the body of your boat.

8.Put the batteries into the dry cell holder.

9.glue the dry cell holder and switch and cover the dry cell holder to prevent water from splashing on it.
